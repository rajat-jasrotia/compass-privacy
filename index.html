Privacy Policy

Smart Compass  ·  Developed by TheTechRJ

Last updated: April 27, 2026

· Overview

Smart Compass ("the App") is a navigation utility developed by TheTechRJ and distributed on the Google Play Store. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

The short version: we do not collect, store, or sell your personal data. Location and camera data stay on your device. The only third-party data involved comes from Google AdMob (advertising) and Google Play Billing (optional donations).

1 Information the App Accesses

Device Sensors

  • Accelerometer and magnetometer — used to calculate compass heading and magnetic field strength. This data is processed entirely on-device and never transmitted anywhere.
  • Camera — used only in Live Compass mode to display a real-world background behind the compass overlay. The camera feed is never recorded, captured, saved, or sent anywhere.

Location (GPS)

  • Fine location (GPS) — used to display your coordinates, altitude, speed, GPS accuracy, and reverse-geocoded street address.
  • Coarse location — used as a fallback when GPS is unavailable.
  • Location data is processed on-device only. It is never transmitted to our servers (we have no servers).
✅ We do not store, log, or share your location or camera data with anyone. There are no accounts, no sign-ins, and no data uploaded to any backend.

2 Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services. Each has its own Privacy Policy and independently governs how it handles data.

Google AdMob Advertising

  • Displays banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads within the App.
  • AdMob may collect device identifiers (Advertising ID), IP address, and general location for ad personalisation, subject to your device's ad settings.
  • You can opt out of personalised ads at any time via your device Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID.
  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

OpenStreetMap (OSMDroid) Maps

  • Provides map tiles for the Map View and Location screens.
  • When loading map tiles online, your IP address may be logged by OSM tile servers in accordance with their standard server logs policy.
  • OpenStreetMap Privacy Policy: osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy

Google Play Billing In-App Purchases

  • Used only if you choose to make an optional donation through the Support screen.
  • All payment processing is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not see, receive, or store any payment or card information.
  • Google Play Terms: play.google.com/about/play-terms

3 Permissions Explained

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION — Required for the GPS Location screen and Live Compass coordinates. Only requested when you navigate to those screens.
  • CAMERA — Required for the Live Compass AR mode. Only active while that screen is open. Never records or saves any footage.
  • INTERNET — Required to load map tiles (OpenStreetMap) and serve ads (AdMob).
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — Allows the App to check connectivity before loading maps or ads.
  • VIBRATE — Used for optional haptic feedback on interactions.
  • BILLING — Required by Google Play to enable optional in-app donations.

4 Data Retention

The App does not retain any user data. There are no accounts, no databases, and no servers operated by TheTechRJ. When you close the App, all sensor readings and location data are discarded from memory.

Map tiles may be cached locally on your device by OSMDroid to allow offline map viewing. You can clear this cache at any time via your device Settings → Apps → Smart Compass → Storage → Clear Cache.

5 Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided personal information through this App, please contact us at the address below and we will take appropriate steps.

The App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Families Policy by not targeting users under 13.

6 Security

Because we do not collect or transmit personal data to any server, there is no external database that could be breached. All sensor and location data is handled locally within the App process on your own device.

7 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App after any change constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
